🔒 Privacy Policy — QPulse
Effective: 24 March 2026
1. Overview
QPulse is a single-page situational awareness dashboard built for residents and expats in Qatar. It aggregates publicly available information from official Qatari government sources, licensed media, and established international news outlets to support daily planning. QPulse is an independent personal project — it is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by the Government of Qatar or any official body.
2. Data We Collect
QPulse does not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information (PII). No login is required. No cookies are set by QPulse itself. Anonymous page-view analytics may be collected via Vercel Analytics to understand usage patterns — this data is aggregated and non-identifiable. Your IP address, location, or browsing history is never stored or shared by QPulse.
3. Data Sources
All situational content displayed on QPulse is drawn from publicly accessible sources including: Qatar News Agency (QNA), International Media Office (IMO), Government Communications Office (GCO), Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MOFA), Ministry of Education & Higher Education (MOEHE), Hamad International Airport (HIA), Qatar Airways, and established regional and international news organisations. QPulse does not create, alter, or fabricate any official government communications.
4. How Data Is Used
The information presented on QPulse is for personal situational awareness and daily planning purposes only. It is not intended as legal, security, or emergency guidance. The AI Summary feature uses the Anthropic Claude API to generate plain-language summaries — no user data is sent to Anthropic; only publicly available situational context is submitted. This tool is designed to support daily planning — not to assess or comment on Qatar's security posture.
5. Data Sharing
QPulse does not sell, share, or transfer any user data to third parties. Third-party links (to news outlets, embassies, airlines, and government portals) are provided for convenience — once you navigate to those external sites, their own privacy policies apply. QPulse is not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites.
6. Contact
For questions, feedback, or requests related to this Privacy Policy or QPulse generally, please contact: seedat.muhammad@gmail.com
